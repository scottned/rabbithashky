Northern Kentucky

Where To Stay
Near Rabbit Hash

Make a weekend of it. Florence and Burlington are 15–20 minutes away and have everything you need.

Rabbit Hash itself doesn't have lodging — the beloved Old Hashienda inn recently closed. But that's no reason to skip an overnight. The Florence/Burlington corridor is 15–20 minutes up the road, well-connected to I-71/I-75, and puts you in easy reach of Rabbit Hash, Cincinnati across the river, and all of Northern Kentucky.

Below are solid options by area. All prices are approximate and vary by season — book early for summer weekends.

Florence & Burlington, KY

Hampton Inn Florence

Florence, KY · ~20 min to Rabbit Hash

Reliable, clean, and well-located off I-75. Easy access to the highway for your morning drive down to Rabbit Hash. Free breakfast, free parking, and the predictability of a name you trust.

Holiday Inn Express Burlington

Burlington, KY · ~15 min to Rabbit Hash

Burlington is the closest full town to Rabbit Hash. A comfortable mid-range stay with easy morning access to the river road. Good for families and motorcycle groups alike.

Cincinnati, OH — Cross the River

Downtown Cincinnati Hotels

Cincinnati, OH · ~35 min to Rabbit Hash

If you're making a long weekend of it, Cincinnati is the full experience — world-class food, Over-the-Rhine, the riverfront, and then a scenic morning drive south into Kentucky for Rabbit Hash.

Newport & Covington, KY

Newport/Covington, KY · ~30 min to Rabbit Hash

Right across the Ohio from Cincinnati, Newport and Covington offer boutique hotels, great dining, and a more laid-back NKY vibe — with easy access to both the big city and the river road south to Rabbit Hash.
