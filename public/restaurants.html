Food & Drink
Near Rabbit Hash

In town and nearby — cold drinks, Kentucky cooking, and a cold beer with a river view.

⚠️ Heads up about Rabbit Hash

Rabbit Hash is a tiny historic hamlet — 254 people, a handful of buildings. Dining options in town are limited to what the General Store is stocking that day. For a proper meal, head to Burlington (~15 min) or Florence (~20 min). We've got the best nearby options below.

In Rabbit Hash

🌮

Chef Hip E's Cocina Loca

Rabbit Hash, KY · Right in town · 4.9★

A BBQ and Mexican eatery right on Lower River Road in Rabbit Hash — tacos, burritos, quesadillas, wings, and sandwiches from a family-run operation that's earned a devoted following. One of the highest-rated food spots in the area. Hours are seasonal and irregular — check their Facebook page before you go.

🏪

Rabbit Hash General Store

10021 Lower River Rd · Est. 1831

Cold drinks, local snacks, Kentucky-made goods, and whatever's behind the counter that day. It's not a restaurant, but it's the only food stop in town — and sometimes that's enough. Grab a cold one, sit on the porch, and watch the world go by at about 10 mph.

Nearby — Burlington & Florence, KY

🍺

Wooden Cask Brewing Company

Newport, KY · ~30 min from Rabbit Hash

Northern Kentucky's craft brewing scene is legit, and Wooden Cask is one of its standouts. River views, solid food menu, rotating taps of well-made ales and lagers. A great reward after your Rabbit Hash visit.

🍽️

Tousey House Tavern

Burlington, KY · ~15 min · 4.6★

Southern cuisine in Burlington's most well-regarded dining room. Warm, casual atmosphere with Kentucky comfort food done right — bourbon selections, fresh ingredients, and service that feels genuinely hospitable. A reliable anchor for your Northern Kentucky evening.

☕

Washington Square Cafe

Burlington, KY · ~15 min · 4.5★

A Burlington institution — American comfort food in a historic setting right on Jefferson Street. Great for breakfast before your Rabbit Hash run, or a relaxed lunch after. The outdoor seating draws a local crowd on nice days.

🍻

Old Guy's Tavern

Burlington, KY · ~15 min · 4.8★

Consistently the highest-rated spot in the Burlington area. A friendly neighborhood grill with cold beer, solid food, and the kind of atmosphere where locals actually hang out. Great conversation, good service — exactly what you want after a day on the river.
